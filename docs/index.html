Dashboardify

A home for the links, notes, and tools you open every day.

Dashboardify is a lightweight dashboard builder: arrange widgets on a canvas, switch between dashboards, and keep everything organized in one place. This current serverless / cloud version stores your data in Google Drive (app data), so your layouts stay with your Google account and follow you across browsers.

  • Bookmarks, notes, images, iframes, countdowns, and more in one view
  • Multiple dashboards for work, personal, or side projects
  • Optional custom CSS when you want to customize the look of the page / widgets
